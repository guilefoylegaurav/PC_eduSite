Frequently Asked Question(FAQS)

What is Pragati Class ?

It is a paid educational platform specially designed to support the educational needs of students belonging to the backward areas of Barak Valley for the initial days , but now it has grown up with an broader initiative to serve the entire state, Assam.

What is the motto of Pragati Classes?

Pragati classes has been started with an initiative of transforming the conventional teaching methods into a modern one,by some B.tech,MBBS,BSc udergraduates for the students belonging to different regions of the state, and most importantly to raise the economically weaker sections by providing education at an affordable price.

Who will teach here?

Students from the NIT, IIT, IISER, SMC, JMC, Assam University and other reputed Institutes will be teaching you here. They are the ones who have already cleared the competitive exams and now will guide you to do the same.

Why should students trust the teachers who are completely new instead of the ones who have been teaching for years ?

We believe conventional learning procedures have become less effective and boring in this modern era, so we tend to bring before you young skilled faculties with effective teaching measures taking care of one's interest

How often do we have to appear for exams? ?

You have to appear once a week for exams and viva voice. This will be divided into two days, i.e., two subjects a day. The exams will be based on the topic taught over the week. The results will be posted within a day or two after the exams.

Can we appear for board type question papers also?

Question papers will be prepared in such a way which will cover both board based questions and competitive as well so that the students don't feel left out as they are not doing a separate preparation for boards exam.
