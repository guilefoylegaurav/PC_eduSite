Being from an under-developed region of Assam myself, We have always harboured the thought that something needs to be done to tap the hidden potential in these promising children. This is the need of the hour. We live in an era of competition, and thus, proper guidance and mentoring have become necessary to ensure that students get to study in the premier institutions of the country. Also, the market has become littered with numerous coaching institutes that lack credibility but charge lack of rupees, and fail to provide proper instruction or deliver satisfaction.

In response to this, Pragati classes was started, with the idea to impart quality education to students. I, along with some of my friends, joined hands and shelled out all the means we could, so that we could bring this novel platform before you – a platform whose inception and operation revolves around the idea that lack of finances should not be a hindrance to the insatiable thirst for wisdom. When Corona was ravaging the nation, we sponsored and backed an amazing platform, "Pragyan Abhiyan", which provided online classes, completely free of cost – which soon became a hit. From 1 June 2020, we officially started our work or mission, the mission of having the students of Pragati classes in every top institutes of not only India but of World.
