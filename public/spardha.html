    • INTRODUCTION



    Education is our Birth Right and whatever be the situation, learning shouldn't stop.

    The motto of Pragati Classes is “Vidwaan sarvatra pujayate” and it means our main motive is to reach every section of society for the holistic development of students by providing quality education. Be it in a rural lane or an urban estate, we aim at reaching every corner to light up the candle of knowledge withing the growing buds of the nation.


    So to keep on track the knowledge of the students, Pragati Classes is organising a fun and exciting event for children studying in Class IX and XII under the banner of 'Spardha'. We are living through the history books as we all know COVID 19 has stopped our daily life in a way never imagined. So, to take that stress and boredom out of students' life for a few days, we are organising this event. The competition is divided into four rounds where the top winners of each round will proceed to the next round. Each round is unique in it's own way with details given below. There are exciting prizes too with cash prizes more than Rs 4000.



    Gear up and go through the stages down the lane.

    EVENTS


    0. ROUND ZERO


    "Spardha" will be launched with an online aptitude test based on general topics which are mentioned below:
    • World History
    • Science and Technology
    • Current affairs
    • Indian Defence
    • Astronomy
    • Logic & Reasoning
    *60 Question in 30 minutes *6 August,2020 6th August 7:00pm - 7:30pm
    Out of the total number of entrants, precisely 48 of them will be selected for the next round.


    Get ready to Enter the real CONTEST now.

    1. ROUND ONE : Antaryuddha


    The top 48 contenders will compete in this round which is comprised of two assessments. The first assesment will be done on the basis of theoretical knowledge, whatever is being taught and learnt in the classes. The second assessment will be done on the basis of capability of applying the textual concepts in real life, which is basically a test of your logical and technical skills.
    The round will be based upon concepts taught in class 11 & 12. So, get ready peeps!
      1. The Theory
      2. Practical Practices
    *60 Question in 60 minutes *6-10 standard questions

    Let's Check your speed now.

    2. ROUND TWO : Fast And Furious


    As the name of the round suggests, it's going to be highly competitive among just a few participants selected. Our excitement for this round couldn't be taken over with just one name for the round so, we have an alias - LAND MINES.

    *Top 24 from round 1 *12 choices will be provided *1 choice can be chosen maximum 2 times *All the winners of Round 2 will get 200 INR cash price

    Just as a land mine is concealed under the ground to hoodwink an enemy to disability, the participants will be trapped in landmines disguised as themes. Each participant have to select a theme of their choice and take the quiz. Once chosen, it can't be changed and the participant has to stick with it. But there's a catch - the highest scoring participant in the previous rounds will get the first chance for selection, following by the next and so on.

    So, get ready for a smart maneuver!

    Time to make real money


    Climb the stairs.

    3. ROUND THREE : Until You Die


    This will be the most challenging round of all as you can guess from it's name where the competitors will fight for the top 6 positions to get into the final. The Round 3 will consists of four sections :
    1. Drishyam : Participants will be asked questions on a video shown.
    2. Mirage : Blurred image will be shown and participants have to guess the correct answer.
    3. You know if you know : A tricky round where you can proceed to the next question if you know the previous answer.
    4. Guess the sound : Participants have to guess the answer from the sound sample
    • Stages:
      1. Drishyam
      2. Mirage
      3. You know if you know
      4. Guess the sound

    Get ready for the Climax.

    4. ROUND FOUR : Antim Parav


    As we all arrive to the last round of this event, there can't be any better name to this round than 'Antim Parav'. This round will be competed by the top 4 contestants who have arrived here beating all the previous participants.
    The main motive of this round is to get to know the participants well. So, it will be an interview round where basic questions of hobbies, future plans, etc. will be asked. After concluding this, the last event is "Sakshatkar" comprising of another personal interview and will be competed by the top 3 participants.
    The interview will be recorded and will be put up in the page of 'Pragati Classes'. The winner will be mainly decided by the Jury by also taking a small consideration of likes and shares.
    Participants reaching round 4 will get INR 500 each

    Check your personality and pressure handling in real time.


    !!!The End!!!
